# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the community
- Showing empathy toward others

**Unacceptable behaviors:**
- Harassment, trolling, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **anian@lmcustoms.cc**.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
